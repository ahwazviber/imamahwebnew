Privacy Policy

imamah is committed to protecting user privacy and handling information responsibly.

1 Overview

imamah is a platform designed to support coordination and engagement across masjids, organizations, and individuals. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the current version of the website and how it may evolve as the platform develops.

2 Current Data Collection

At this stage, the imamah website does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information from users browsing the site.

3 Email Communication

If you choose to contact imamah via email, including through inquiry links, any information you provide will be used solely to respond to your message and maintain communication regarding your inquiry.

imamah does not sell, share, or distribute personal information provided through email.

4 Analytics

Basic analytics may be used to understand general website usage and improve user experience. These analytics do not collect personally identifiable information.

5 Information We May Collect (Future Features)

As the imamah platform develops, certain features may require the collection of limited information, such as:

  • Account and profile details
  • Location-related data for map-based features
  • User-submitted content, such as posts, events, or community contributions

Any such data will be used strictly to provide, maintain, and improve the platform.

6 User-Generated Content

Users may have the ability to submit content within the platform. imamah reserves the right to review, moderate, or remove content that violates community guidelines, applicable laws, or platform standards.

7 Data Use and Protection

imamah is designed with a minimal and responsible data approach. Any information collected will be used only for operational purposes and platform improvement. Reasonable measures will be taken to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse.

8 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the platform evolves. Continued use of imamah indicates acceptance of any updates to this policy.

9 Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or general inquiries, please contact:
